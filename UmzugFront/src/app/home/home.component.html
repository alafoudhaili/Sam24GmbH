We raise efficiency, accuracy, transparency, and flexibility with a partner centric proposition (shipper and carrier) to permit a fair journey for each user within XsupplY. Our value propositions:
For shippers:
-Have access to all offers and carriers at the same time.
-Guarantee the availability of space for shipment.
-Compare prices and offers.
-Simplified processes (decision, invoicing, etc).
-Trace their goods.
-Evaluate suppliers and their quality of service.
-Consolidate all needs on the same portal and have a single point of contact.
-Extract reports and analyze data collected through the platform.
For carriers:
-Optimize their cargo.
-Decrease their empty returns.
-Save money on their used assets and resources.
-Reach more customers.
-Digitize their documents and process.
